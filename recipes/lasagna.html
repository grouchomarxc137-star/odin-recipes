Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, also known by the plural form lasagne, is a type of pasta made in very wide, flat sheets.

In Italian cuisine it is used to make the dish lasagna by stacking layers of pasta, alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

  2. Make the ricotta filling
  3. Preheat the oven
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Repeat the layers and bake
