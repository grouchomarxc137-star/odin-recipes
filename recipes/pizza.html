Pizza

Description

Pizza is an Italian dish consisting of a flattened, leavened bread dough topped with ingredients like tomato sauce, cheese, and various other toppings , then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

It can be described as a savory, baked flatbread with a wide variety of toppings, including fresh vegetables, meats, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Add tomato sauce
  3. Add mozzarella
  4. Add extra toppings
  5. Toss it in the oven
